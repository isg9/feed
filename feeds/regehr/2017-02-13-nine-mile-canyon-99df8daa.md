# Nine Mile Canyon

One of my boys and I spent Sunday exploring Nine Mile Canyon, in the remote [Book Cliffs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Book_Cliffs) a few hours drive from Salt Lake City. This canyon is known for its dense collection of rock art and ruins, a lot of which can be seen from the paved road that follows the canyon bottom. This was a lot of driving for a day trip but it wasn’t too boring since I had checked out an audiobook of *Fahrenheit 451* from the library.

A great pictograph panel, the figures are several feet high.
