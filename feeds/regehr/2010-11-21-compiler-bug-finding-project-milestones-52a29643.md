# Compiler Bug-Finding Project Milestones

Despite the Fall semester being crappy for me (double course load, and I keep getting sick) this project has made good progress:

- Xuejun gave a talk at the [LLVM Developer’s Meeting](http://llvm.org/devmtg/2010-11/)
- We submitted our 300th compiler bug report (up from [200 in March 2010](https://blog.regehr.org/archives/39))
- We submitted a paper to PLDI 2011 summarizing our methods and results; I’m keeping fingers crossed that I won’t have to burn an effigy of the dreaded [reviewer 3](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-VRBWLpYCPY)

The next milestone will be to release Csmith, our test-case generator, as open source, hopefully before Christmas.
