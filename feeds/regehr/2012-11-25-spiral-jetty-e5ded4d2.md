# Spiral Jetty

Smithson’s [Spiral Jetty](http://www.diaart.org/sites/page/59/1245) is often totally submerged, or else high and dry as it was when I last saw it. Today a favorable [lake level](http://waterdata.usgs.gov/ut/nwis/uv/?site_no=10010100&PARAmeter_cd=00065,00060,00010,72020) as well as warm weather and a feeling of having spent maybe one too many days around the house motivated us to take a day trip. The hazy air and calm water created an otherworldly effect. Afterwards, we stopped by the [Golden Spike site](http://www.nps.gov/gosp/index.htm) and also the [Thiokol rocket garden](http://www.roadsideamerica.com/tip/16594).
