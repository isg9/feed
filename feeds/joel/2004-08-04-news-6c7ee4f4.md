# News

[Eric Sink](http://software.ericsink.com/entries/No_Great_Hackers.html): “It‘s okay to be in awe of these great hackers. But as a practical matter, small ISVs would be much better off hiring professionals.” Agreed!

Which reminds me, I can’t wait for [Johanna Rothman’s](http://www.jrothman.com/) new book on hiring geeks to come out. I’m reading the manuscript now and it’s a great book on a topic that doesn’t get a lot of coverage.
