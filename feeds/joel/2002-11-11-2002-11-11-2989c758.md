# 2002/11/11

Abstractions fail. Sometimes a little, sometimes a lot. There’s leakage. Things go wrong. It happens all over the place when you have abstractions, and it’s dragging us down. Read all about it in my latest feature article: [The Law of Leaky Abstractions](https://www.joelonsoftware.com/articles/LeakyAbstractions.html).
