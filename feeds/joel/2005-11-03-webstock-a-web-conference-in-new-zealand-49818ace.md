# Webstock: a Web Conference in New Zealand

I’m looking forward to speaking at [Webstock](http://www.webstock.org.nz/), a web conference next May in Wellington, New Zealand.

[![Webstock](https://i0.wp.com/www.joelonsoftware.com/wp-content/uploads/2005/11/03Webstock.png?w=730&ssl=1)](http://www.webstock.org.nz/)

This will be fun. I haven’t been to New Zealand since 1987. It’s such a long trip from New York City (something like 24 hours, I think) that I really should to try to combine this trip with some other meetings or conferences in the South Pacific / Australia / New Zealand area.
