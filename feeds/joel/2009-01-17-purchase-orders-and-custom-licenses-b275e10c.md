# Purchase orders and custom licenses

Over on the Business of Software group, we’re [discussing](http://discuss.joelonsoftware.com/default.asp?biz.5.724788.12) those big bureaucratic companies who want a custom legal agreement to buy $79 worth of software. “Lawyer-infested companies that are obsessive-compulsive about contracts do not pirate software and they CERTAINLY don’t try to trick you into signing a contract ‘allowing’ them to pirate your software. This theory is laughable.”
