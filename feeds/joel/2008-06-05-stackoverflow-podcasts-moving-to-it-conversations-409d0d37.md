# StackOverflow podcasts moving to IT Conversations

Yes! I’m still doing those weekly podcasts with [Jeff](http://www.codinghorror.com/). We’ve already done eight of them.

![](https://i0.wp.com/www.joelonsoftware.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/06/05stackoverflow.png?w=730&ssl=1)We’re moving, though, to [IT Conversations](http://itc.conversationsnetwork.org/index.html), a huge network of terrific audio shows about technology. Just looking at all the great shows they have there makes me feel a bit like a kid in jeans and a T-shirt with a dirty slogan who just walked into Chez Panisse.

The new feed, IT Conversations-based feed is at [http://rss.conversationsnetwork.org/series/stackoverflow.xml](http://rss.conversationsnetwork.org/series/stackoverflow.xml).

The easy way to subscribe is with ITunes, choose Advanced \| Subscribe to Podcast, paste that URL in there, and you’ll be all set.
