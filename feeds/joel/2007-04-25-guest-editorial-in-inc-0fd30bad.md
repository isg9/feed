# Guest editorial in Inc.

Inc. (the magazine) is a great resource for entrepreneurs. For the last several years, I’ve been reading it cover-to-cover the day it arrives.

That’s why I’m incredibly honored that they invited me to write [a guest editorial about recruiting and internships](https://www.joelonsoftware.com/inc.html?4) in this month’s issue. Thanks to professional editing, it feels a little bit polished compared to my usual style. I don’t think I would write, “Ah, college.” I do remember writing, “Get me a frosty cold orange juice, hand-squeezed, and make it snappy!”
