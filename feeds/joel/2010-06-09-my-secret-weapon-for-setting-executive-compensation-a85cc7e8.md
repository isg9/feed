# My secret weapon for setting executive compensation

My friend [Noam](http://founderresearch.blogspot.com/) at Harvard Business School does annual surveys of executive compensation in tech companies. The surveys are a great way to figure out how much to pay that VP of Sales you were thinking of hiring, or whether your equity grant as CTO is fair.

You can [buy](https://compstudy.com/user/register) the studies for $999, or you can [participate in the survey](https://compstudy.com/) and get free access to the results when they’re published.
