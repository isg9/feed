The only harbinger of what was to come was that I could sense a growing irritation in myself towards Jeff Atwood. Why? Because Jeff just couldn’t keep up with the pace of Joel’s conversational tennis.”

Ha! Take that, Jeff “Atwood,” if that’s even your real name, you homicidal maniac!
