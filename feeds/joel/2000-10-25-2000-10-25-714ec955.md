# 2000/10/25

**[**Another Business Model That Doesn’t Seem To Work**](https://www.joelonsoftware.com/articles/fog0000000032.html)**

I used to have [Atomz.com](http://www.atomz.com/) as my search provider. When Atomz asked me to start paying ($100 for three months), it took me 20 minutes to switch over to [Google](http://www.google.com/services/). Kiss them goodbye.

**Nortel’s Loss is Fog Creek’s Gain**

As soon as the investment community realized that [Babak Ghahremanpour](http://babak.weblogs.com/) had left Nortel to join [Fog Creek Software](http://www.fogcreek.com/), Nortel’s stock [plunges 27%](http://cnnfn.cnn.com/2000/10/25/technology/nortel/).
