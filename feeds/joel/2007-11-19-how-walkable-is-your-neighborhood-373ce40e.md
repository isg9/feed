# How walkable is your neighborhood?

This is cool… my friend Mike Mathieu set up a web page where you can [enter an address and find out how walkable it is](http://www.walkscore.com/).

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.joelonsoftware.com/wp-content/uploads/2007/11/19walk.png?w=730&ssl=1)](http://www.walkscore.com/)

[Microsoft’s campus](http://www.walkscore.com/get-score.php?street=1+microsoft+way%2C+redmond%2C+wa&go=Go) gets a 31 (“For most errands, driving or public transportation is a must.”).

The [Googleplex](http://www.walkscore.com/get-score.php?street=+1600+Amphitheatre+Parkway%2C+mountain+view%2C+ca&go=Go): 34\. Good thing Google gives you a few bucks towards a Prius.

The [Fog Creek office](http://www.walkscore.com/get-score.php?street=535+8th+Ave%2C+New+York%2C+NY&go=Go): 98 out of 100. (“Most errands can be accomplished on foot and many people get by without owning a car.”)

Conclusion: [Ditch your car, work at Fog Creek](http://www.fogcreek.com/Careers.html).
