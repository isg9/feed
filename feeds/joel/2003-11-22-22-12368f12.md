# 22

**Tidbits**

My incoming spam is running at over 200 junk emails a day, but [SpamBayes](http://spambayes.sourceforge.net/) is catching them all, with virtually no false positives. [Bayesian filtering](http://www.paulgraham.com/spam.html), invented by Paul Graham and available in many open source implementations, is the best answer yet.
