# 2002/01/08

New on the Fog Creek web site: [CityDesk for Geeks](http://www.fogcreek.com/CityDesk/fog0000000068.html).

Dave Thomas 1932-2002![Dave Thomas](https://i0.wp.com/www.joelonsoftware.com/wp-content/uploads/2002/01/daveThomas.jpg?resize=163%2C174&ssl=1)

Dave Thomas, the founder of Wendy’s, passed away today. Appearing in hundreds of commercials, he gave his hamburger chain a friendly, down-home feel. “Mr. Thomas came to stand for the products he peddled because he was perceived as standing behind them, as if a curious customer could peek inside the kitchen at a Wendy’s restaurant and perhaps find Mr. Thomas keeping a watchful eye on the cook at the grill,” wrote [Stuart Elliot](http://www.nytimes.com/2002/01/09/business/media/09ADCO.html) in the New York Times. He taught us that nobody, given the choice, prefers to buy from a faceless megacorporation.
