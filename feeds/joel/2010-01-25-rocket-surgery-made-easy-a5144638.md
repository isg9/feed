# Rocket Surgery Made Easy

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.joelonsoftware.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/25Krug.png?w=730&ssl=1)](http://www.sensible.com/rocketsurgery/index.html)[Steve Krug](http://www.sensible.com/) has written a follow up to his usability classic [*Don’t Make Me Think*](http://www.sensible.com/dmmt.html) *.* The sequel, [*Rocket Surgery Made Easy*](http://www.sensible.com/rocketsurgery/index.html) *,* is a terrific, short, concise, fun guide to running simple “hallway” usability tests to improve the usability of your software and websites. Highly recommended.
