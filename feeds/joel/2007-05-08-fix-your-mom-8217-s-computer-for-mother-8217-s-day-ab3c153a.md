# Fix your mom&#8217;s computer for mother&#8217;s day

![](https://i0.wp.com/www.joelonsoftware.com/wp-content/uploads/2007/05/08help.jpg?w=730&ssl=1)This Sunday is Mother’s Day. Why not fix your mom’s computer?

You know: remove the spyware and adware, install [Firefox](http://www.mozilla.org/firefox), and make it so that weird toolbar toast doesn’t pop up every 15 seconds.

To make it easy, this Sunday we’re making [Fog Creek Copilot](https://www.copilot.com) absolutely free.

No strings attached. Just go to [https://www.copilot.com](https://www.copilot.com) on Sunday, get a free pass, and we’ll email your mom a link she can click on to download the helper application. It’s really easy.

P.S. Same deal applies for Father’s Day, June 17.
