# Kathy Sierra’s talk at the Business of Software 2009

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.joelonsoftware.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/10kathy-thumbnail.png?w=730&ssl=1)](https://i0.wp.com/www.joelonsoftware.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/10kathy.png?ssl=1)I’m a long-time Kathy Sierra fan. Her talk at the Business of Software 2009 was amazing, even though, in retrospect, I realize now that she had a slide making fun of Stack Overflow’s terrific new VC-backed business model. She even got the [unicorn](http://meta.stackoverflow.com/questions/37328/my-godits-full-of-unicorns)!

Watch the  whole talk [here](http://blog.businessofsoftware.org/2010/05/kathy-sierra-at-business-of-software-2009.html). [The next Business of Software conference](http://www.businessofsoftware.org/index.aspx) will be in Boston, October 4th-6th.
