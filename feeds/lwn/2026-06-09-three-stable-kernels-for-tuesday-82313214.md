# Three stable kernels for Tuesday

Greg Kroah-Hartman has announced the release of the [7.0.12](https://lwn.net/Articles/1077079/), [6.18.35](https://lwn.net/Articles/1077080/), and [6.12.93](https://lwn.net/Articles/1077081/) stable kernels. Each contains important fixes throughout the tree. Users are advised to upgrade.
