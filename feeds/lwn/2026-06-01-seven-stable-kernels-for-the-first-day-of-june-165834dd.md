# Seven stable kernels for the first day of June

Greg Kroah-Hartman has announced the release of the [7.0.11](https://lwn.net/Articles/1075807/), [6.18.34](https://lwn.net/Articles/1075808/), [6.12.92](https://lwn.net/Articles/1075809/), [6.6.142](https://lwn.net/Articles/1075810/), [6.1.175](https://lwn.net/Articles/1075811/), [5.15.209](https://lwn.net/Articles/1075812/), and [5.10.258](https://lwn.net/Articles/1075813/) stable kernels. As usual, each contains important fixes throughout the tree, including a [fix](https://lwn.net/ml/all/2026060140-CVE-2026-46243-3d1c%40gregkh/) for the " [CIFSwitch](https://heyitsas.im/posts/cifswitch/)" vulnerability ( [CVE-2026-46243](https://cve.org/CVERecord/?id=CVE-2026-46243)) which could allow a local-privilege-escalation exploit. Users are advised to upgrade.
