# Fedora F44 election interviews published

The Fedora Project has [published](https://communityblog.fedoraproject.org/f44-elections-interviews/) interviews with candidates running for the open seats on the [Fedora
Council](https://docs.fedoraproject.org/en-US/council/), [Fedora Engineering
Steering Committee](https://docs.fedoraproject.org/en-US/fesco/), [Fedora
Mindshare Committee](https://docs.fedoraproject.org/en-US/mindshare-committee/), and [EPEL
Steering Committee](https://docs.fedoraproject.org/en-US/epel/epel-policy-steering-committee/). Voting is [open](https://elections.fedoraproject.org/) through Friday, June 12 at 23:59 UTC.
