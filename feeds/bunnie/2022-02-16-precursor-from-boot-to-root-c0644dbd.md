# Precursor: From Boot to Root

I have always wanted a computer that was open enough that it *can* be inspected for security, and also simple enough that I *could* analyze it in practice. [Precursor](https://precursor.dev) is a step towards that goal.

![](https://bunniefoo.com/precursor/precursor_boot_to_root.png)

As a test, I made a one hour video that walks through the Precursor tech stack, from hardware to root keys. I feel it’s a nice demo of what evidence-based trust should look like:

The video is a bit of a firehose, so please refer to our [wiki](https://github.com/betrusted-io/betrusted-wiki/wiki) for more info, or [open an issue](https://github.com/betrusted-io/betrusted-wiki/issues) to further the discussion.

*Erratum #1:* I had mistakenly attributed [SpinalHDL](https://spinalhdl.github.io/SpinalDoc-RTD/master/index.html) as a subset of Chisel. SpinalHDL is actually a separately developed HDL by Charles Papon. It was developed contemporaneously with Chisel and inspired by many concepts in it, such as using Scala as the underlying language; but it is not affiliated with Chisel.
