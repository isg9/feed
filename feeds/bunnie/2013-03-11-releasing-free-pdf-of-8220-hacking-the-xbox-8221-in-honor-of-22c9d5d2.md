# Releasing free PDF of &#8220;Hacking the Xbox&#8221; in honor of Aaron Swartz

No Starch Press and I have decided to release a [free ebook version of Hacking the Xbox](http://nostarch.com/xboxfree) in honor of Aaron Swartz. As you read my book, I hope that you’ll be reminded of how important freedom is to the hacking community and that you’ll be inclined to support the causes that Aaron believed in.

[![](http://nostarch.com/sites/default/files/xboxnew.jpg)](http://nostarch.com/xboxfree)

*Apologies in advance if there are any hiccups in the distribution of the free copy. The primary host is a server in my flat, which only has a 100Mbps up-link (currently pushing >30Mbps one hour after posting).*
