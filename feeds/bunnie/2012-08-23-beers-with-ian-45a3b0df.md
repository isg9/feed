# Beers with Ian

I had the fortune of kicking it with Ian Lesnet from [Dangerousprototypes.com](http://dangerousprototypes.com) and having a couple of beers in my workshop while watching Curiosity stick its landing on Mars (massive props to the JPL and NASA engineers who pulled that off, btw!).

Ian took a video of the meet-up and with [Chris Gammell’s](http://theamphour.com) help did a bang-up job of editing it. The [interview is posted at his site](http://dangerousprototypes.com/2012/08/23/workshop-video-36-beers-in-bunnies-workshop/), and in addition to giving a quick tour of my workshop we traded tips on soldering, sourcing, and survival in the Shenzhen electronics markets. Too bad the interview was done before my [new laser cutter](http://fslaser.com/products/newhobby) arrived…it would have been fun to engrave silly pictures on random household items!
