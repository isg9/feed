# Maker Faire and Hardware Innovation Workshop!

I’m looking forward to next week — one of my favorite events, [Maker Faire](http://makerfaire.com/), is just around the corner, and so is the [Hardware Innovation Workshop](http://makezine.com/hardware-innovation-workshop/agenda-2012.html).

I’ll be making the trek from Singapore this weekend so I can host a [“Master Class Workshop”](http://makezine.com/hardware-innovation-workshop/master-class-workshop.html) at the Hardware Innovation Workshop on the morning of May 14. Also in the line-up are Zach “Hoeken” Smith ( [haxlr8r](http://www.haxlr8r.com/)), John Park ( [AQS](http://www.aqs-inc.com/index.html)), James “Laen” Neal ( [OSH Park PCB](http://oshpark.com/)), and Zack Kaplan ( [Inventables](https://www.inventables.com/)), all of whom will share valuable insights and perspectives on manufacturing and scaling up.

We’ll be getting down to brass tacks with a group of Makers who are looking to scale their big ideas. We’ll share experiences about trying, and often times failing, to get things made. And we’ll finish with a set of campfire discussions, where Makers will get a chance to ask any questions weighing on their mind. If you liked my series of posts on [“The Factory Floor”](http://www.bunniestudios.com/blog/?cat=19), you might also enjoy [this workshop](http://hiw2013.eventbrite.com/?access=WSHOP#).
