# Little Brother

I thought I’d give a shout-out to Cory Doctorow and his very successful new novel, [“Little Brother”](http://craphound.com/littlebrother/about/), for which I had the privilege of writing an afterword. The book has spent four weeks on the New York Times best seller list and I highly recommend it to the readers of this blog; if you liked my book [Hacking the Xbox](http://hackingthexbox.com/) you’ll definitely like this one. Pick it up at [Amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com/Little-Brother-Cory-Doctorow/dp/0765319853) or [download it](http://craphound.com/littlebrother/download/) — it’s Creative Commons licensed!

Congrats again to Cory, I am so stoked for your hard-earned success!
