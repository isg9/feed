# Fun Times

So I promised a bunch of my friends I’d post some photos from this past weekend up on my blog (remember, this is my personal blog, not the [company site](http://bunniestudios.com)!). Started the weekend at Pana’s birthday party at none other than [Rei Do Gado](http://entertainment.signonsandiego.com/profile/168113) (yum!), where I got to meet some [new friends](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/treo_071505_010.jpg), witness [Dom’s O face](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/treo_071505_008.jpg), and subsequently suffer [food coma](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/treo_071605_001.jpg) at hb’s place.

Next day, I had a blast at the [SD Comicon](http://www.comic-con.org/index.php). Lots of [crazy costumes](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/pixie_chicks.jpg), some of them quite elaborate and well executed, as well as various celebs and [booth oddities](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/camera_guy.jpg).

![](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/bunnie_eekers.jpg)

I got to meet Eekers from the [frag dolls](http://www.fragdolls.com/blog.php?doll=eekers) at the [PA](http://www.penny-arcade.com) booth; a very pretty, nice girl, down to earth and apparently friggin awesome at gaming. Rad!

I also got to pose among various Star Wars live-action figures:

[![](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/darth_bunnie_sm.jpg)](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/darth_bunnie.jpg)

Jeff did an awesome job! He’s super-talented and I was totally blown away by the art at his booth.
