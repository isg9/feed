# See you at 28c3

I’ll be giving a talk at [28c3](http://events.ccc.de/congress/2011/Fahrplan/day_2011-12-29.en.html) this year on December 29 at 6:30PM about a Man in the Middle attack on HDCP that I discretely embodied within the [NeTV](http://www.kosagi.com/w/index.php?title=What_is_NeTV). The sharp fellows at rootlabs noticed the trick and disclosed the attack in [a blog post from September](http://rdist.root.org/2011/09/13/the-magic-inside-bunnies-new-netv/), which was further elaborated by [The Register](http://www.theregister.co.uk/2011/09/16/chumby_nettv_hack/print.html). I will disclose all the naughty bits about the attack’s implementation in my talk this December.

Now that I have departed chumby, I will produce an open-source version of NeTV to support developers & OEMs through my Singapore-registered company, [Sutajio Ko-Usagi](http://kosagi.com). Adafruit.com is currently [taking pre-orders](https://www.adafruit.com/products/609). Thank you Phil and Limor for taking the time during peak season to get the product up on your website! You guys rock.

Stay tuned for more…
