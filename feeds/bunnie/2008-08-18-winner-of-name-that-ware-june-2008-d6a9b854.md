# Winner of Name that Ware June 2008!

The ware for June 2008, provided courtesy of xobs, is a “Kitchen Sync” expansion card from the ’90s, used with the [Video Toaster](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Video_Toaster). The Kitchen Sync is a sync generator for video signals, and it includes two independent time base correctors.

I love retro hardware like this — brings me back to the day when garage startups could really build and market hardware products that made a difference. Thanks for playing!
