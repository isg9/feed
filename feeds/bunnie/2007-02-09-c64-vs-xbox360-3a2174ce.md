# C64 vs. Xbox360

Nate Lawson (co-designer of the Blu-Ray content protection layer) had a nice presentation at RSA2007 [comparing the content protection schemes used on the Commodore64 vs. the Xbox360](http://root.org/talks/RSA_20070206.pdf). His slides are an interesting and fun read…kind of brings out a certain nostalgia, but then you realize that things aren’t all that different today.
