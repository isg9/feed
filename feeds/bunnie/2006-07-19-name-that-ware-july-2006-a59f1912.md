# Name that Ware July 2006

The ware for July 2006 is shown below. Click on the image for a much larger version.

[![](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/july_2006_sm.jpg)](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/july_2006.jpg)

I was walking around [Halted Specialties](http://www.halted.com/) about a week ago before my talk at the National Youth Leadership Forum. Halted Specialties is one of my favorite places to go and kill an hour when I’m in between meetings in silicon valley, lots of neat wares to look at and learn from. I saw this gem on the shelf and I had to buy it–I had heard of these, but never seen one of these in real life before. Honestly, I don’t know exactly what this does or what it was used for, but I do recognize that this is a very special kind of logic. To see this, check out the “wiring” that it uses:

[![](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/july_2006_back_sm.jpg)](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/july_2006_back.jpg)

I am looking forward to people’s guesses on this one!
