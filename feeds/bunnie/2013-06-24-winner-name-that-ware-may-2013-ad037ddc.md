# Winner, Name that Ware May 2013

The winner for May 2013 hails from the [Evil Mad Science blog](http://www.evilmadscientist.com/2013/may-ware-winne/), by the (apropos) handle of VAX-Dude. Congrats, email me for your prize!

The Ware is a VAX9000 High Density Signal Carrier, and [Windell’s write-up](http://www.evilmadscientist.com/2013/may-ware-winne/) on how the carriers are built and work is quite a good read. It reminds me that there’s always more than One Way to make a circuit substrate. In fact, the process still sounds like something viable and worth looking into even today for very high density interconnects that require a bendable substrate.
