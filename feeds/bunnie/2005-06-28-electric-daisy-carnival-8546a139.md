# Electric Daisy Carnival

So this past weekend I went to my first massive in LA. I had heard about these west-coast raves back when I was at MIT going to the “dinky” 1,000-person events in New England, so I was really excited and I didn’t know what to expect. It was everything I thought it would be and more! I have no idea how many people were there, but it had to be at least 10,000+. The music was great (I really enjoyed [Infected Mushroom’s](http://infected.co.il) set, and surprisingly, I really enjoyed [Ferry Corsten’s](http://www.ferrycorsten.com/) set as well. I thought I was all burned out on progressive trance but he really managed to rock the decks and throw down a thrilling set).

I ended up going with [Caustik](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/edc/Electric_Daisy_Carnival_028.jpg) ( [homepage](http://www.caustik.com/)), [Brian](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/edc/Electric_Daisy_Carnival_011.jpg), and [Christine](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/edc/Electric_Daisy_Carnival_015.jpg) (fun, cute chick from Bavaria)–all folks who work for [DivX](http://www.divx.com/). The carnival was interesting…lots of free carnival rides, various carnie foods, [fire](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/edc/Electric_Daisy_Carnival_025.jpg), booths vending all things music related, and of course, lots of [rad music](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/edc/Electric_Daisy_Carnival_030.jpg), [eye candy](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/edc/Electric_Daisy_Carnival_005.jpg), and [colorfully](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/edc/Electric_Daisy_Carnival_033.jpg) [dressed](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/edc/Electric_Daisy_Carnival_022.jpg) [people](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/edc/Electric_Daisy_Carnival_023.jpg). I think we [dan](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/edc/Electric_Daisy_Carnival_021.jpg) [ced](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/edc/Electric_Daisy_Carnival_019.jpg) for about six hours straight, although it was pretty [hot](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/edc/Electric_Daisy_Carnival_032.jpg) at times. Brian and Caustik got to go back stage and [meet one of the DJ’s](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/edc/Electric_Daisy_Carnival_034.jpg) as well. The atmosphere at the place reminded me at times of my trip to Ibiza. It was a [hella good](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/edc/Electric_Daisy_Carnival_007.jpg) time.

More pics at [Brian’s website](http://www.gotfudge.net/gallery/ElectricDaisyCarnival?page=1)! Brian gets all the photo credits for this post. Thanks man!
