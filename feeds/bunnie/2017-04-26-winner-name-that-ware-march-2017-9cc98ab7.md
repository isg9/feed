# Winner, Name that Ware March 2017

The ware for March 2017 seems to be a Schneider ATV61 industrial variable speed drive controller. As rasz\_pl pointed out, I left the sticker unredacted. I had a misgiving about hiding it fearing the ware would be unguessable, but leaving it in made it perhaps a bit too easy. Prize goes to rasz\_pl for being the first to guess, email me for your prize!
