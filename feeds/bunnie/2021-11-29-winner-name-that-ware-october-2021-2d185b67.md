# Winner, Name that Ware October 2021

The Ware for October 2021 is a [TFT liquid crystal display](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Liquid-crystal_display#Active-matrix_technologies) (with any luck, the image I uploaded & added to the entry was accepted by the Wikimedia editors). Congrats to Joe for guessing it first, email me for your prize! The ware itself was a pretty easy guess, but I was inspired to share it because the quality of the photography really spoke to me. The first thought that came to my head when I saw it was, “this should be in the encyclopedia entry for liquid crystal displays”. So, with [jackw01](https://jackw01.github.io)‘s permission, I’ve tried to make it happen!
