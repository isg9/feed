# Name that Ware April 2009

The Ware for April 2009 is shown below. Click the photo for a much larger version.

[![](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/ntw_apr_09_sm.jpg)](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/ntw_apr_09.jpg)

This is a platitude, but using a flatbed scanner produces awesome results on circuit boards. Somehow over the years I never got around to buying a flatbed scanner that I didn’t [take apart](http://www.bunniestudios.com/blog/?page_id=51), but recently a coworker put an old one up for grabs. I actually wasn’t the first one to claim it but thankfully the first claimant was a Mac-only guy and the scanner only had drivers for the venerable PowerPC Macs. Well, his loss is your gain, at least for wares with only low-profile components. The scans are good enough to image process and use automated image recognition techniques to pull out connectivity; makes me want to delaminate a circuit board and give it a try.
