# Name that Ware, July 2022

The Ware for July 2022 is shown below.

[![](https://bunniefoo.com/ntw/ntw_july_2022_sm.jpg)](https://bunniefoo.com/ntw/ntw_july_2022.jpg)

This is yet another fine ware contributed by [jackw01](https://jackw01.github.io). I kind of like how the board on the left went straight from bringup to production, without any attempt to strip out the debug headers or JTAG ports. Which is totally fine, btw: why change something that already meets spec, and might even be helpful with improving yields?…I just have to resist the urge to plug something into said ports.
