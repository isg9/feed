# Solution, Name that Ware October 2017

It’s unusual to find a ware without a clear winner, and reading through the comment thread I found a lot of near-misses but none of them close enough for me to declare a winner.

The [Ware for October 2017](https://www.bunniestudios.com/blog/?p=5013) is a Minibar Systems automated hotel minibar (looks like a “SmartCube 40i”, “The Minibar of the Future”). During an overnight layover, I decided to check the minibar for snack options, but upon pulling what I thought was the handle for the fridge, lo and behold a tray of electronics presented itself.
