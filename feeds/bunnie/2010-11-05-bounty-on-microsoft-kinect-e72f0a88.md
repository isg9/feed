# Bounty on Microsoft Kinect

Adafruit is hosting an “X-prize” style competition where they are offering a $2,000 bounty for anyone who can create an Open-Source driver for the Microsoft Kinect game peripheral (reminds me of [MR’s Xbox Linux prize](http://news.cnet.com/2100-1040-978957.html) but on a smaller scale). Lots of details about the competition and what Kinect is at [this link](http://www.adafruit.com/blog/2010/11/04/the-open-kinect-project-the-ok-prize-get-1000-bounty-for-kinect-for-xbox-360-open-source-drivers/).

Sounds like a fun project, just wish I had the time!
