# Chumby&#8217;s Launched!

Well, time for the shameless plug. You’ve probably seen the posts on [how the hardware is manufactured](http://www.bunniestudios.com/blog/?cat=7). Finally, after much hard work by the whole team, the [Chumby](http://www.chumby.com) is officially launched! If you’ve been waiting to get one, now’s as good a time as any other to [buy one](http://store.chumby.com/). As part of launch, the [chumby website](http://www.chumby.com) got a face-lift, and the [launch firmware](http://www.chumby.com/pages/latest_software) includes a lot of nice new features, bug fixes, and usability enhancements.

[CNET](http://reviews.cnet.com/digital-media-receivers/chumby-black/4514-6739_7-32853285.html?part=cnet&subj=Chumby+(black)) and [Gizmodo](http://gizmodo.com/359388/hands-on-chumby-wi+fi-widget-beanbag-cuddly-in-more-ways-than-one) already have reviews of the chumby up. Chumby also has a set of [launch partners](http://www.chumby.com/partners), including CBS, Food Network, MySpace, MTV Mobile, Shoutcast and the Weather Channel. These launch partners, along with a growing community of independent widget developers, have contributed to the [hundreds of widgets available](http://www.chumby.com/guide) now for the Chumby.
