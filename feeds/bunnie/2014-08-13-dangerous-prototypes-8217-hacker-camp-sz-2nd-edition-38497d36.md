# Dangerous Prototypes&#8217; Hacker Camp SZ, 2nd Edition

My buddies at [Dangerous Prototypes](http://dangerousprototypes.com/2014/08/07/hacker-camp-shenzhen-vol-2-reflux-open-for-registration/) are hosting another Shenzhen hacker camp at the end of September. If you missed the last hacker camp or are just curious about Shenzhen, [check it out](http://dangerousprototypes.com/2014/08/07/hacker-camp-shenzhen-vol-2-reflux-open-for-registration/) — the slots are filling up fast!

> Come to the world’s electronics capital and experience Shenzhen like a local hacker. Tour the famous Huaqiangbei electronics markets with people who live in the neighborhood, figure out what to eat and how to get around, and of course – learn how to reball BGA chips from a soldering master with noth’n but hand tools.
>
> Optional: Tuesday 23 – early arrival dinner at Japanese Secret Location Optional: Wednesday 24 – tour of Dongmen market & sign street, copy mall Thursday 25 – talks: how to survive Shenzhen, Huaqianbei tour Friday 26 – talks: how to use Shenzhen to the fullest, BGA reballing day 1 Saturday 27 – BGA reballing day 2, hacker BBQ
>
> That’s just an overview. See the [full Hacker Camp Shenzhen schedule here](http://dangerousprototypes.com/hackercampshenzhen/schedule/). You can expect nightly dinners and parties all week. If you want to come really early, we’re hacking Phuket from the 15th to the 19th.
