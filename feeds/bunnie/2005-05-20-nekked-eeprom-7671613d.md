# Nekked EEPROM

As part of the Tandy 102 hack, I couldn’t resist taking a couple of zoomed-in shots of the EEPROM silicon. You can see them [here](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/tandy/eeprom_zoom.jpg) and [here](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/tandy/eeprom_megazoom.jpg). You can clearly see the AMD logo in one of them, and in the other one you can see the indivdiual transistors and gates that perform some of the logic functions on the EEPROM.
