# Winner, Name that Ware August 2011

The Ware for August 2011 is a circuit board from a [Micromass (now Waters) Quattro LC](http://photos.labwrench.com/manuals/4130-1219.pdf) triple-quadrupole mass spectrometer. The winner is Keenan, for making such a keen guess (sorry, couldn’t resist) despite not knowing much about the field, narrowing the board down to being part of a quadrupole mass spec analyzer. Congrats, email me to claim your prize!
