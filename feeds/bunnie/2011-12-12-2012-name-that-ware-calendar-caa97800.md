# 2012 Name That Ware Calendar

By popular request, I’ve compiled all the wares from the 2011 Name that Ware season and put them into a wall calendar for 2012. This will be available to next year’s winners of Name that Ware as one of the prize choices, or you can buy one yourself at the [bunniestudios cafepress shop](http://www.cafepress.com/bunniestudios.602339797).

Each month pictures the featured ware from the previous year, annotated with the identity of the ware and the competition winner … with the exception of December, of course, as this month’s competition has yet to conclude!
