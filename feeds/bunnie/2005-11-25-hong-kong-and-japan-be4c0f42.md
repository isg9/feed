# Hong Kong and Japan!

I’ve been out of town for the past two weeks, which is why this month’s name that ware contest is late. I decided I needed a vacation after the big tape-out and a bunch of other important deadlines, so [hb, caustik, str1d3r and I](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/japan05/hk_backbus.jpg) headed out for the big .hk and .jp for a little asian adventure. It was a rollicking good time. It was my first time to Hong Kong. It’s an [interesting place with a beautiful skyline](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/japan05/hk_skyline.jpg), but I’ll have to say it’s not my favorite place to visit–the food wasn’t as good as I had hoped it to be, and the city is a little dirty/seedy in some places, although the shopping is pretty good if you’re into that kind of thing. We met up with [Alex Kampl and the Lik-Sang crew](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/japan05/alex_caustik.jpg) while we were out there, which was a lot of fun–thanks to Alex for showing us a good night out, and giving us a [tour of the Lik Sang facilities](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/japan05/hk_topbldg.jpg)!

Japan was a great time as always. This was my fifth time out in Japan, and we went to Asakusa (where we stayed at the [Shigetsu Ryokan](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/japan05/ryokan.jpg)), Ginza ( [Sony building](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/japan05/sony_bldg.jpg)), Shinjuku (all around great city, [home base](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/japan05/shinjuku.jpg), and home to many [good restaraunts](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/japan05/imahan.jpg)), Harajuku ( [Takeshita Dori](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/japan05/takeshita.jpg), great people-watching spot), Shibuya ( [Xbox 360 Lounge](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/japan05/xbox360.jpg) and the main [Shibuya drag](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/japan05/shibuya.jpg)), Roppongi ( [clubbing (saw Yoji Biomehanika live at Velfarre)](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/japan05/yoji.jpg) and [bar-hopping](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/japan05/roppongi.jpg)), Akihabara (electronics town, of course!), Yoyogi Park ( [cosplay](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/japan05/yoyogi.jpg) and people watching on a Sunday), [Tsukiji Fish Market](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/japan05/tsukiji.jpg), Odaiba, and the [Ooedo Onsen Monogatari](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/japan05/ooedo2.jpg) ( [a hot-bath theme park](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/japan05/oodeo.jpg), which is a really great way to wind up a long trip). There were too many adventures to list here, but it was a great time…Tokyo is an awesome city!

Cosplay in Yoyogi park, おもしろい　ね!
