# OSHW v0.4 Definition Released

The conclusion of the [Open Source Hardware Summit](http://www.openhardwaresummit.org/) has yielded a new [draft definition for open source hardware v0.4](http://freedomdefined.org/OSHW_draft) as well as a draft statement of principles. Participate and add your comments [here](http://www.openhardwaresummit.org/forum/viewtopic.php?f=5&t=76)! For your browsing pleasure, [Make](http://blog.makezine.com/archive/2010/10/open-source_hardware_-_around_the_w.html) also has a great collection of perspectives on the new definition.
