# Winner, Name that Ware December 2012

The Ware for December 2012 are boards from a [2100MHz RF PA module with digital predistortion](http://emailwire.com/release/WiseBand-Communications-Ltd-Announces-Wise2100-MCPA-for-UMTS-Basestations.html), from a defunct company named Wiseband.

[![](http://bunniestudios.com/blog/images/wiseband_6166601.png)](http://www.google.com/patents/US6166601)

The submitter of the ware notes that there is a patent that is a close hit on this design, see figure 4 of “ [Super-linear multi-carrier power amplifier](http://www.google.com/patents/US6166601)“. The PA board is block 108, the digital board is block 124; the rf board, with the 3 downconverters and 1 upconverter, is block 120, with some components connected externally to the board (not seen in the scans) – the delay lines and the error amplifier (154, 156).

Picking a winner this time is like throwing darts — lots of insightful analysis, but no exact matches. I’ll go with…Andy Bardagjy, email me for your prize!
