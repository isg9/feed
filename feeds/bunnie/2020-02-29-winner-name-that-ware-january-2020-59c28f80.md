# Winner, Name that Ware January 2020

The ware for January 2020 is the brains of an [IQ Motion Control position module](https://iq-control.com/iq-position-module). Congrats to Cody for guessing it, email me for your prize! And yes, I did [blog about the modules](https://www.bunniestudios.com/blog/?p=5215) a couple years ago, and since then I’ve found them to be an indispensable element of my mechatronic design toolbox. I’m just finishing up on delivering another successful design using these, so I had a couple extra laying about to take apart. If you’re doing any kind of motion control or robotics, it’s [worth checking them out](https://www.crowdsupply.com/iq-motion-control/iq-motor-module).
