# Name that Ware May 2013

The Ware for May 2013 is a special guest ware hosted by my friends at [Evil Mad Scientist](http://shop.evilmadscientist.com/), purveyors of fine electronic curios, such as the [Eggbot](http://shop.evilmadscientist.com/productsmenu/tinykitlist/171-egg-bot), the clever [bulbdial](http://shop.evilmadscientist.com/productsmenu/tinykitlist/156) clock, and a wall-sized version of [Conway’s Game of Life](http://shop.evilmadscientist.com/productsmenu/majors/476).

[![](http://farm4.staticflickr.com/3716/8893311814_3ea5bbab0d.jpg)](http://www.evilmadscientist.com/2013/may-ware/)

[![](http://farm8.staticflickr.com/7388/8892701331_31ace81bc0.jpg)](http://www.evilmadscientist.com/2013/may-ware/)

When I was visiting their shop in Sunnyvale sometime last year, Windell showed me this curious ware. It was one of the most fabulously constructed circuit boards I had ever seen, and I asked if he wouldn’t mind sharing it as a guest ware for my blog. Please check out his [site](http://www.evilmadscientist.com/2013/may-ware/) for more photos! I’ll be checking comments posted both at Evil Mad Scientist and here for winners.
