# Today is remembrance day

On the radio today they were addressing the fact that young people today aren't taught enough Canadian History to be able to talk meaningfully about Canada's involvement in WWI. This is hilarious to me because of the unstated premise that any Young Person Today (or Old Person Today) has the slightest idea what WWI was about in the first place. Does it matter more that Canada sent some troops to die in the mud in Belgium, or that the entire world order was reshaped?
