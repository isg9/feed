**guadec**

Tomorrow I fly to Birmingham for [GUADEC](http://www.guadec.org/). I'm very much looking forward to catching up with old people, meeting new ones, and poking various ideas. Also, I am looking forward to a GUADEC without responsibilities -- no video archives to haunt me into the fall.

**debt**

I upgraded to development GNOME a month or so ago; perhaps the most notable difference to me is [gnome-power-manager](http://www.gnome.org/projects/gnome-power-manager/) dimming my display more or less intelligently, and its very nifty power consumption graphs. [Richard Hughes](http://hughsient.livejournal.com/) does great work and should drink for free this GUADEC!

**lexical closure**

Hackers loving Emacs must read the wonderful [org-mode tutorial](http://dto.freeshell.org/notebook/OrgTutorial.html).
