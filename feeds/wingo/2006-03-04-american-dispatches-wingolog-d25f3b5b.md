# american dispatches — wingolog

## [american dispatches](/archives/2006/03/04/american-dispatches)

4 March 2006 8:42 PM

- [random](/tags/random)

It has been quite the week, this ninth of 2006. Excuse the boringly sober prose below, I attribute it to the concrete jungle outside the window.

Sunday. A Belgian arrival on an overnight bus from snowboarding in [Tignes](http://www.valdisere.com/gb/domaine/description/killy.php) (pics to come soon\[0\]). Pieces of a [FOSDEM](http://www.fosdem.org/2006) Sunday, went out a microbit in Gent with some of [Thomas](http://thomas.apestaart.org/log/)' friends \[1\]. Good folks, very alive.

Monday. Back on the train, plane from Brussels to Barcelona. Took a [Catalan test](http://www.cpnl.cat/), and out to [aikido](http://aikifeilen.com/). It seemed my instructor there had found me a [flat out in Sant Andreu](http://www.bcn.es/cgi-guia/guiamap4/cgi-guia/?actives=&scl=5&cnt=32345.60%2C87307.88&plt=32345.60%2C87307.88&plant=capes4e&txt1=&txt2=C.+de+Coroleu%2C+1&idioma=1&grayscl=0&escala.x=39&escala.y=28), so arranged to go and see it.

Tuesday. Back to work. Left early to go to a [Guadec](http://beta.guadec.org/) planning [meeting](http://mail.gnome.org/archives/guadec-list/2006-March/msg00000.html) and drinks. Packed up all my stuff from what was home for my year in Barcelona.

Wednesday. Worked late, preparing for next week. Afterwards with Wim's help I moved out, and so closed a chapter on 4% of my life. Then out to dinner, skipped the bar to go back to [Christian](http://blogs.gnome.org/uraeus)'s, where I'm staying now.

Thursday. Out early to see the flat -- didn't look so good, but I managed to find an agency staffed by non-psychopaths which is a rarity. Into work and working late, picked up Catalan test results at midday -- 96%, not bad at all. Asked out my catalan teacher. Proposition declined!

Friday. Up before 5 to catch a plane out of Barcelona through Frankfurt, [too much snow](http://news.airwise.com/story/view/1141384293.html) \-\- the flight from BCN couldn't land, so I missed my direct flight to Portland. They rebooked me through Denver, but after getting on the plane we sat on the runway for *7 hours*.

Saturday. Woke up in a Denver hotel at 5 local time. My internal clock is a bit off. The waitress at breakfast is a drug pusher. My hands jitter from the cofffee.

This week leaves me like a riverbed in a rainstorm, events coursing through the perceptual venturi that is me. It's not going to stop soon either. On Monday I start a week facilitating 40 hours of training classes. After a redeye back to Barcelona next Monday I have tickets to Belfast to investigate St. Paddy's day with my sister that lives there. Moonlight sleeping on a midnight lake eh.
