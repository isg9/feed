Edward Tufte [speaks and writes about the iPhone](http://www.edwardtufte.com/bboard/q-and-a-fetch-msg?msg_id=00036T). Interesting stuff. Tufte's radical perspectives on interface are quite interesting to me as a marginal player in the computing world\[1\].

The things I do are a bit odd, off-beat, but that's fine: I don't have many institutional constraints. I can build any kind of pixel combination that I want\[2\]. So onward minimal infocentric interfaces, and onward those hacking for unchanneled goals.

**verses, favorite**

I am terribly, terribly ignorant regarding poetry. Still, in reference to verse: WCW's [This is just to say](http://www.mat.upm.es/~jcm/williams-this.html). Yeats' [The second coming](http://www.potw.org/archive/potw351.html). And regarding the rest: ignorance. Unknown territories worthy of exploration? Please comment.

\[1\] radical: *[of or going to the root or origin; fundamental](http://dictionary.reference.com/browse/radical)*
