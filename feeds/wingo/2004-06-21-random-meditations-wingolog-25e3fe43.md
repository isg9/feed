**whiteness**

The greeting for the eveningtime in Oshiwambo literally says, "did it become white for you?" What really happens is that everything fades away but the sky, so it's like the world of light above, and the world of people bumping into things below. Then the stars come out and the whiteness reverses, moonlight and the milky way reflecting off the sand.
