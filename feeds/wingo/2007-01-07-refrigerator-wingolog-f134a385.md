Off to Éire for christmas, where I was promptly whisked off into the [countryside](//wingolog.org/photos/index.py/tags/killowen/) and stuffed full of food. My sister seems to be doing all right too.

After a day back in Barcelona I caught another plane to Madrid, landed in Barajas' Terminal 4 just a few hours after [ETA blew up the parking deck](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2006_Madrid_Barajas_International_Airport_bombing). Aside from the explosions I dig on Madrid, a fine town.

I have more to write about, but in the interests of ["worse is better"](http://www.dreamsongs.com/WorseIsBetter.html), this will be my offering for now.
