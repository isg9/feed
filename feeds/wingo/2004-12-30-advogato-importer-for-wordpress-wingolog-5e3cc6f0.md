## [advogato importer for wordpress](/archives/2004/12/30/advogato-importer-for-wordpress)

30 December 2004 10:01 AM

- [meta](/tags/meta)
- [advogato](/tags/advogato)

[Advogato's](http://advogato.org/) RSS export only takes you about 10 entries back. If you want older ones, you'll need to scrape them from the web, or otherwise use the [XML-RPC interface](http://advogato.org/xmlrpc.html).

I was going to do this manually, but there were too many entries, and I hadn't hacked anything for a few weeks, so instead I took the opportunity to write an [Advogato importer for wordpress](//wingolog.org/pub/import-advogato.php.txt). Just grab it, drop it in the `wp-admin` dir, point your browser to `blogurl/wp-admin/import-advogato.php`, and follow the directions.

While doing this I [proved myself to be a moron](http://advogato.org/person/wingo/diary.html?start=95).
