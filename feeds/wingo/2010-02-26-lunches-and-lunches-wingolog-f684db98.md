There are lunches, and then there are lunches to write about.

Today I started to think I was in one of the latter when the [patxaran](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Patxaran) came out; but I knew it when I decided to go to the park afterwards and lay down in the sun. Tomato & oil, chard & potato, rare beef, sweet fried things, vermouth, wine, ["rancid wine"](http://ca.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vi_ranci), and yes, a shot of espresso.

Also: Barcelona's Ciutadella is its [Dolores Park](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dolores_Park). No [bi-rite creamery](http://biritecreamery.com/), nor [tartine bakery](http://www.tartinebakery.com/), but the park itself has that same feel, sometimes. Wafting guitar, bagpipes, plucked violin, tightrope walkers, and jugglers, hand and foot.
