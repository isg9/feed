**NAME**`git-brunch` \- Fresh fruit before lunch

**SYNOPSIS**`git brunch [-p]`

**DESCRIPTION**With no arguments, stashes all work and cherry-picks a snack onto the dining room working tree. Option `-p` runs a `git-prune-snacked(1)` afterwards.

**NOTES**If running `git-brunch` does not produce the desired effects, try with `sudo`. For external brunches, see `git-remote`.

**BUGS**Objects received via `git-fetch-snack(1)` should be inspected manually to ensure their fitness for brunch. Strawberries, cream, and waffles are currently unimplemented.

**SEE ALSO**`git-cherry-pick(1)`, `git-snack-objects(1)`
