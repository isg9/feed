**word**

> Equanimous \\E\*quan"i\*mous\\, a.
>
> \[L. aequanimus, fr. aequus equal + animus mind.\]
>
> Of an even, composed frame of mind; of a steady temper; not easily elated or depressed.
>
> \[1913 Webster\]

Delightful. Makes me think of [Medialens folk](http://medialens.org/cogitations/080216_non_violence_and.php).

**words**

I have a new favorite orator: [Michael Parenti](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_Parenti).

A brief search on the interwebs will only find you [his web site](http://www.michaelparenti.org/), which is a bit off-putting, and doesn't really show to his strengths. But take a listen to [Land of Idols](http://mbanna.radio4all.net/pub/archive2/MP3/parenti-land_of_idols.mp3) (40 minutes), then check out the other talks in [that same directory](http://mbanna.radio4all.net/pub/archive2/MP3/) (search for "parenti").

**wheels**

Set off on what must have been a [100 km loop](http://www.gmap-pedometer.com/?r=2078065) last Sunday on the bike, after coming back from GUADEC; Google thinks the distance was significantly less. Perhaps I should report a bug?

Meanwhile at home, [yet](//wingolog.org/archives/2007/06/27/mixed-nuts) [another](//wingolog.org/archives/2006/08/21/sittin-in-a-park-in-paris-france) broken spoke.

**sense**

It was so quiet walking home this evening that even smelling the street odors made me feel like I was eavesdropping.
