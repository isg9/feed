**bike**

[![](//wingolog.org/photos//2006/9/22/DSC01397.normal.JPG)](//wingolog.org/photos/index.py/tags/camino)

Just finished a most excellent trans-spain bike trip, starting from France, heading along the northern coast of Spain, cutting in across the mountains in Asturias, ending in the northwestern state of Galicia.

[![](//wingolog.org/wp-content/camino-videolog.png)](//wingolog.org/movies/camino.m3u)

I kept a dorky videolog of the trip as well; click the above for a playlist file containing about 25 short clips I recorded with my digital camera. It's about 20-30 minutes in total, and about 35 megabytes. Older (more than a month ago) GStreamer+totem should play it fine; if you have trouble viewing the files, maybe try a different player (mplayer for example). Or alternately here's the [directory with the movie files](//wingolog.org/movies/camino/).
