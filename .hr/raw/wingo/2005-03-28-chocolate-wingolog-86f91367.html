Decided to paint my bedroom this weekend. Three walls a light color, one a dark color, and somehow I got fixated on brown for the latter. As the first smudges started to smear across my white wall, the question was obvious:


¿mierda?

I got a bit discouraged for a while, pouted for a bit, then figured that since I already bought the paint that I should finish the wall. So I did, and I rather like it. It's earthy.

Halfway through painting, I realized that my bedroom was turning into ubuntu colors (the other walls are a light yellow). Amusing. Ubuntu did turn me more towards brown as a color.
